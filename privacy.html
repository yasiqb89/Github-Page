Website Privacy Notice

Last updated 14 July 2026 · Applies to ryzlo.net

This notice covers this website. The Ryzlo iOS app is covered separately by the app privacy policy. The app keeps your Screen Time and focus data on your device, and this website never receives it.

Who we are

Ryzlo is the data controller for the personal data described here.

Data protection contact: ryzloapp@gmail.com

What we collect, and why

WhatWhyLawful basis
Your email address, if you join the waitlist To email you once, when Ryzlo launches Consent
Your name, email and message, if you use the contact form To read and reply to what you sent us Consent
Aggregate page analytics · pages viewed, referrer, country, device type To understand which pages people find useful Legitimate interests
Server logs, including your IP address Automatically created by our host to deliver the site and keep it secure Legitimate interests

We do not buy, sell, rent or share your data for marketing. We do not build profiles, and we do not track you across other websites.

Cookies

This website sets no cookies. There are no advertising cookies, no tracking pixels, and no cross-site tracking, which is why you were not asked to accept any.

Our analytics (Cloudflare Web Analytics) is cookieless: it does not set a cookie, does not fingerprint your device, and does not follow you to other sites.

The only thing we store in your browser is a single session-only entry (ryzlo-intro) that remembers the opening animation has already played, so you don't sit through it twice. It contains no identifier, is never sent to us, and your browser discards it when you close the tab.

Fonts

Our fonts are served from our own servers rather than a third-party font CDN, so loading this page does not disclose your IP address to a font provider.

How long we keep it

  • Waitlist email: until you unsubscribe, or until 12 months after launch, whichever comes first. Then it is deleted.
  • Contact messages: up to 12 months after we have answered you, then deleted.
  • Analytics: retained by Cloudflare in aggregate form. It is not linked to you.

Your rights

Under the UK GDPR and EU GDPR you can ask us to:

  • give you a copy of the data we hold about you;
  • correct it if it is wrong;
  • delete it;
  • restrict or object to how we use it;
  • send it to you, or another provider, in a portable format.

Where we rely on consent, you can withdraw it at any time. Every email we send carries a one-click unsubscribe link, and you can also just email us. Withdrawing consent does not affect anything we did before you withdrew it.

Email ryzloapp@gmail.com. We'll get back to you as quickly as we can, and within one month at the latest.

If you think we have got it wrong, you can complain to the UK's Information Commissioner's Office at ico.org.uk, or to the data protection authority where you live.

Changes to this notice

If we change how we handle your data, we will update this page and change the date at the top.
