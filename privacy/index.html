Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how FocusApp handles information when you use the FocusApp mobile application.

Information We Collect

FocusApp is designed to work without requiring you to create an account. Information you add in the app, such as focus activity, preferences, and app settings, is stored on your device unless a platform feature you control, such as device backup, sync, or restore, stores it through Apple services.

Subscriptions and Purchases

Paid subscriptions and in-app purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. FocusApp does not receive or store your payment card information. Subscription management, cancellation, billing, refunds, and renewal settings are handled through your Apple ID and App Store account settings.

Analytics and Diagnostics

FocusApp may receive crash reports, diagnostics, and performance information from Apple when you have chosen to share analytics with app developers in your device settings. This information is used to fix bugs and improve reliability.

How We Use Information

Information handled by FocusApp is used to provide app functionality, remember your preferences, maintain subscription access, improve app reliability, and respond to support or privacy requests.

Sharing

FocusApp does not sell your personal information. Information is not shared with third parties for advertising. Limited information may be processed by Apple as needed for App Store purchases, subscriptions, device services, diagnostics, backups, or other Apple platform features you use.

Children's Privacy

FocusApp is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Choices

You can delete app data by deleting the app from your device. You can manage subscriptions in your Apple ID account settings. You can control whether Apple shares diagnostics and analytics with app developers in your device privacy settings.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated when material changes are made.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, use the support contact listed for FocusApp on the App Store.
